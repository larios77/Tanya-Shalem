Services

We provide quality and regulatory expertise that helps medical device teams develop clear pathways toward compliance. Our support is structured, practical, and aligned with international requirements.

Regulatory Strategy & Submission Support

We help teams build and implement regulatory strategies suited to their product type and target markets, including U.S. FDA, EU MDR/IVDR, and additional global regions. Support may include planning, submission management, documentation preparation, and coordination with internal stakeholders.

Quality System Development & Optimization

Tanya Shalem LLC assists organizations in establishing, refining, and maintaining quality systems that align with standards such as ISO 13485, QSR, and MDSAP. Areas of support include system structure, process development, documentation alignment, and readiness for audits or certification activities.

Auditing Services

We conduct internal audits, supplier audits, and mock-certification assessments. Findings are documented clearly to help teams understand gaps, address issues, and strengthen long-term compliance.

Technical Documentation & Remediation

Support includes development, organization, and review of technical documentation essential for regulatory submissions and audits. Gap assessments help teams prioritize updates and maintain alignment with current requirements.

Design Controls, Risk Management & Transfer to Production

We guide teams in setting up or refining design control frameworks, maintaining risk management documentation, and preparing for transfer-to-production activities in line with recognized regulatory expectations.

Post-Market Surveillance & Vigilance

We help companies establish and manage post-market systems, monitor product performance, and fulfill reporting obligations as required for their device type and markets.

Interim & Ongoing QA/RA Leadership

For teams in transition or expanding capacity, we can provide temporary or ongoing leadership support, ensuring continuity across quality and regulatory functions.
