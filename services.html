Services

We provide structured quality and regulatory support designed to help medical device teams establish clear pathways toward compliance while supporting efficient product development and lifecycle management.

Regulatory Strategy and Submission Support

We develop regulatory strategies tailored to your product risk profile and target markets. Our team manages the end to end submission process for the U.S. FDA, EU MDR/IVDR, and other global regions, ensuring technical documentation is robust and submissions are coordinated seamlessly across all stakeholders.

Quality Management System Excellence

We help you establish and maintain quality management systems aligned with ISO 13485, QMSR, and MDSAP. From process optimization to audit readiness, we build flexible frameworks that ensure compliance while supporting your operational efficiency and long term growth.

Product Realization and Digital Compliance

We align design controls, risk management, and requirements traceability with your R&D milestones, ensuring that validation and verification tests support your submissions. We focus on a robust design transfer process, establishing a precise Device Master Record (DMR) that ensures full-scale and compliant manufacturing. We provide technical leadership for Computerized Systems Validation (CSV), ensuring full alignment with 21 CFR Part 11 and GAMP 5 principles.

Operations and Manufacturing Quality

We provide hands-on support for the transition to full-scale production. This includes managing process validation activities (IQ/OQ/PQ), labeling and UDI controls, and distribution and service quality. We treat your supply chain as part of your quality system by managing supplier qualification, quality agreements, and targeted audits.

Auditing, Remediation, and Post-Market Surveillance

We conduct rigorous internal, supplier, and mock certification audits to identify and close compliance gaps. Our support extends through the entire post-launch phase, managing remediation plans, CAPA, and post-market surveillance systems. We provide direct support for communications with regulatory authorities to ensure continued product safety and compliance.

Strategic Capability Building and Leadership

We provide specialized executive leadership coaching to develop quality and regulatory leaders, equipping them with practical strategies to foster a strong compliance culture and build high-performing teams. For organizations in transition, we also provide interim Q&R leadership to maintain stability and continuity.
