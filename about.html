About

Tanya Shalem LLC is a specialized consulting firm dedicated to quality and regulatory affairs for the medical device and life science industry. We support companies at various stages of growth by translating regulatory requirements into practical, actionable strategies that align with business objectives.

Our Mission

To guide medical device and life science organizations through complex quality and regulatory requirements with clarity, integrity, and precision enabling the development and commercialization of safe, effective, and compliant technologies.

Our Approach

Clear and Actionable

We translate complex regulations into structured, manageable steps. By providing the technical clarity your team needs, we ensure you can make informed decisions and maintain steady progress toward market access.

Collaborative and Committed

We act as a seamless extension of your organization. Grounded in 18 years of technical and executive experience, we don’t just consult; we take ownership of your most critical quality and regulatory milestones.

Integrated and Scalable

We provide a unified Quality and Regulatory suite tailored to your company’s specific size and stage. By aligning global strategy with your business goals, we build flexible systems that support your innovation and grow with you.

Tanya Shalem

CEO and Head of Quality & Regulatory Affairs

I believe that regulatory requirements shouldn’t be a barrier to innovation. They should be the framework that ensures your medical advancements reach the world safely and efficiently.

As the lead strategist and the founder of Tanya Shalem LLC, I bring over 18 years of experience guiding companies through FDA submissions, EU MDR transitions, QMS implementations, and global regulatory requirements. My expertise centers on complex multidisciplinary devices, specifically diagnostic imaging that integrates hardware, software, and deep learning algorithms.

With a B.Sc. in Quality Engineering and a Master of Engineering in Industrial Engineering and Management from the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, I work closely with technical teams to translate complex business and regulatory objectives into practical engineering steps, keeping your project moving toward its milestones.

Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, my team and I help medical device and life science companies to bring new technologies to market, build strong and efficient processes, and guide suppliers and internal teams while maintaining a high level of regulatory compliance.
