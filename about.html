About

Tanya Shalem LLC is a specialized consulting firm dedicated to quality and regulatory affairs for the medical device industry. We support companies at various stages of growth by translating regulatory requirements into practical, actionable strategies that align with business objectives.

Our Mission

To guide medical device organizations through complex quality and regulatory requirements with clarity, integrity, and precision, enabling the development and commercialization of safe, effective, and compliant technologies.

Our Approach

Clear & Actionable

We simplify complex regulations into structured steps that support progress, readiness, and informed decision-making.

Collaborative & Supportive

We work as an extension of your team, fostering open communication and dependable partnership.

Grounded in Industry Experience

Our guidance is informed by extensive, hands-on experience across quality systems, regulatory submissions, audits, and post-market activities.

Tanya Shalem | CEO and Head of Quality & Regulatory

Tanya Shalem brings extensive experience leading and supporting quality and regulatory functions for medical device companies across multiple global markets. Her background includes quality system development, regulatory strategy, submission management, auditing, technical documentation, and post-market compliance. She is known for providing structured guidance and practical interpretation of regulatory expectations that teams can confidently implement.
