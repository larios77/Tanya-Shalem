About

Tanya Shalem LLC is a specialized consulting firm focused on quality and regulatory affairs for the medical device industry. We provide practical, strategic support designed to help teams interpret requirements, strengthen quality systems, and prepare products for global markets.

Our Mission

To guide medical device organizations through the complexities of regulatory and quality requirements with clarity, integrity, and precision—so they can deliver safe, effective products to patients worldwide.

Our Approach

Clear & Actionable

We break down complex regulatory language into structured steps that support progress and readiness.

Collaborative & Supportive

Clients work with a partner who values open communication and creates a dependable environment for teams at any stage.

Grounded in Industry Experience

Decades of hands-on regulatory and quality experience inform every recommendation, ensuring solutions that align with real-world operations.

About Tanya Shalem

Tanya brings extensive experience leading and supporting quality and regulatory functions for medical devices across multiple global markets.
