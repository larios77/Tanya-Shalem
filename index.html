About Us

Navigating Complexity: Practical Solutions, Vision, and Integrity

Tanya Shalem LLC provides quality and regulatory consulting led by Tanya Shalem and a team of skilled professionals. We partner with MedTech and Life Science companies to turn complex global requirements into a clear path for growth. With a foundation in engineering and over 18 years of experience, we provide hands-on execution for worldwide submissions, including FDA and CE Mark, alongside robust QMS implementation per ISO 13485, FDA QMSR, MDSAP and EU MDR requirements.
We focus on the alignment between technical innovation and compliance by specializing in customized solutions based on your organization’s specific needs, products, and size. Whether managing regulatory and quality projects, gap assessments, remediations, or direct communication with Notified Bodies and the FDA, we work as an integral part of your team. Partners choose us because we combine deep technical mastery with a commitment to integrity and leadership, ensuring you not only secure market access but also build a resilient culture of quality that lasts.
